Some more about me!

I started swimming at a very young age. I always loved being in the water, whether it was taking a bath or going to my grandmother's house or to church pool parties. I have always had swimming to fall back on as something fun and exciting, and I wish that everyone could have that experience. I haven't always been the fastest or most gifted at swimming, but I have leveraged my knowledge of the sport and my work ethic to win. I would love to help your swimmer succeed, whether that is learning to be safe in the water or learning complex techinque drills and strokes. If you want to hear more about my experience with swimming, my blog has some more info.

Why DanielSwim?

I believe that DanielSwim is able to provide something different and unique compared to most other swim lesson companies. I am a swimmer myself; I am still having practices and learning new things every day. I use what I learn to make my teaching better. Other companies do not spend as much time on each individual student; I am able to spend more time on each student and learn more about them.
