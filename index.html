A bit about me!

  • Current freshman at Vanderbilt University in Nashville, Tennessee studying computer science and math
  • 1000+ hours of experience teaching swim lessons to 200+ kids
  • American Red Cross CPR certified
  • 3-year Southlake Carroll Varsity swimmer; won state in 2022 season
  • Member of Vanderbilt's Swim Club
  • Very comfortable with children of various ages
  • Competitive student in academics and athletics at Vanderbilt (3.81 GPA, National Merit Scholar)

What to expect:

  • 1 to 2 week swimming camps, personalized based on your child's abilities and needs
  • One-on-one lessons in the comfort of your own pool
  • For younger children: I will teach basics of freestyle and help with making them love the water
  • For older and more experienced children: I will teach basics of all strokes and keep them loving swimming
  • Introduction to competitive swimming concepts; advanced swimmers will be ready for club/stroke team swimming!
  • Ability to teach all four strokes, turns, and introduce the joy of competitive swimming
  • I will bring a kickboard, dive toys, sunscreen and other materials to teach drills and have fun
